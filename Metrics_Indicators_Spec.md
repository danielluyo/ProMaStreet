# Metrics & Indicators Specification

## Overview
This document defines the key performance indicators and metrics relevant for Delivery Managers and Project Managers in consulting environments. The indicators focus on four major dimensions: Financial, Project, Team, and Client. These metrics aim to provide visibility, predictability, and actionable insights for managing complex client engagements.

---

## 1. Financial KPIs
Financial KPIs focus on revenue performance, profitability, budget adherence, and contract integrity.

### Revenue & Profitability
- **Revenue-to-Date vs Forecast**: Measures billed revenue against the financial plan.
- **Gross Margin per Project**: Evaluates profitability after delivery costs.
- **Projected Margin**: Forecasted margin for the full duration of the project.

### Cost Control
- **Budget Burn Rate**: Speed at which the project budget is consumed.
- **Actuals vs Budget Variance**: Difference between planned and actual spending.
- **Non-Billable Hours Percentage**: Indicates hidden costs and efficiency gaps.

### Contract Performance
- **Utilization per Pricing Model**: Monitoring client usage across Time & Materials, Fixed Price, or Hybrid contracts.
- **Revenue Leakage**: Hours worked but not billed.
- **Change Request Capture Rate**: Effectiveness in converting scope changes into revenue.

---

## 2. Project KPIs
Project KPIs assess delivery predictability, quality, risk management, and schedule adherence.

### Delivery Health
- **Overall RAG Status**: High-level indicator summarizing project health.
- **Timeline Adherence**: Percentage of milestones completed on time.
- **Scope Stability**: Frequency and impact of scope changes.

### Execution Predictability
- **Velocity & Velocity Stability**: Measures consistency of delivery across sprints.
- **Throughput vs Plan**: Comparison of completed work units to expectations.
- **Rework Rate**: Effort spent correcting prior work.

### Risk & Issue Management
- **Open Risks by Severity**: Snapshot of current risks and their impact.
- **Open Issues by Severity**: Outstanding issues affecting delivery.
- **Escalation Count**: Frequency of problem escalations.

### Quality Indicators
- **Defect Density**: Number of defects relative to work delivered.
- **UAT Pass Rate**: Success rate of user acceptance testing.
- **Release Stability**: Frequency of issues after deployment.

---

## 3. Team KPIs
Team KPIs monitor staffing efficiency, morale, productivity, and collaboration.

### Staffing & Capacity
- **Team Utilization Rate**: Percentage of time team members contribute to productive client work.
- **Capacity vs Demand**: Balance between available capacity and project workload.
- **Role Coverage**: Having all critical roles properly staffed.

### Team Health & Stability
- **Turnover / Attrition Rate**: Stability and retention within the team.
- **Backfill Time**: Time required to replace team members.
- **Team Morale Score**: Derived from surveys or internal assessments.

### Performance & Output
- **Productivity per Role**: Delivery output relative to expectations for each role.
- **Skill Mix Alignment**: Match between team skills and project needs.

### Engagement & Collaboration
- **Responsiveness to Client**: Time taken to respond to client communication.
- **Internal SLA Adherence**: Meeting internal timelines (e.g., code reviews, requirement clarifications).

---

## 4. Client KPIs
Client KPIs evaluate relationship health, engagement quality, alignment, and account potential.

### Relationship Health
- **Client Satisfaction Score (CSAT)**: Direct client feedback rating.
- **Net Promoter Score (NPS)**: Client willingness to recommend the service.
- **Engagement Sentiment Score**: Observed positivity or concern during interactions.

### Engagement Behavior
- **Client Responsiveness**: Timeliness of client replies.
- **Requirement Clarity Score**: Quality and clarity of requirements received.

### Governance & Alignment
- **Steering Committee Action Closure Rate**: Percentage of governance decisions successfully completed.
- **Decision Turnaround Time**: Speed at which client decisions are made.
- **Dependency Blocker Count**: Impact of client-side dependencies.

### Account Value Indicators
- **Upsell / Cross-Sell Potential**: Opportunities for additional services.
- **Renewal Likelihood**: Probability of contract extension.
- **Relationship Coverage**: Strength of engagement across executive, tactical, and operational levels.

---

## 5. Composite KPIs
Composite KPIs are aggregated indicators that provide holistic visibility across multiple delivery dimensions.

### Delivery Predictability Index
A combined score based on timeline adherence, velocity stability, defect trends, and risk exposure.

### Account Health Score
An overall assessment integrating financial status, delivery performance, client sentiment, and governance coverage.

### Team Health 360
A multi-dimensional score factoring in utilization, morale, attrition risk, and skill alignment.

---

## Purpose
These KPIs support proactive management, enabling delivery leaders to:
- Identify risks early.
- Improve predictability.
- Strengthen client relationships.
- Drive profitable and high-quality delivery.
- Make informed staffing and scaling decisions.
