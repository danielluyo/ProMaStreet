## üìä Team Member Data Structure

### Fields for Each Team Member

#### **Basic Information**
- **Name**: Full name
- **Project Role**: Role on this specific project
- **General Role**: Overall position in company
- **Seniority Level**: JUNIOR / MID / SENIOR / LEAD / PRINCIPAL / ARCHITECT
- **Email**: Contact email
- **Phone**: Contact phone number

#### **Skills & Expertise**
- **Primary Skill**: Main area of expertise
- **Skills Array**: All technical/functional skills (6+ displayed)
- **Certifications**: Professional certifications and credentials
- **Languages**: Spoken languages

#### **Tenure & Experience**
- **Years in Company**: Total company tenure
- **Months in Project**: Time on this specific project
- **Performance Rating**: 1-5 stars

#### **Availability & Utilization**
- **Availability Status**: FULL_TIME / PART_TIME / CONSULTANT / ROTATING
- **Utilization Percentage**: % of time allocated to this project
- **Hours Per Week**: Weekly commitment

#### **Location & Timezone**
- **Location**: City, State/Country
- **Timezone**: Full timezone with UTC offset
  - Example: "EST (UTC-5)", "IST (UTC+5:30)", "CET (UTC+1)"

#### **Notes**
- PM notes about the team member's strengths, working style, or important context
